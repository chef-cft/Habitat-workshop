Decentralized Deployments

When working with a diverse edge fleet, many additional requirements may impact how we deploy applications.

  • Do you have franchised locations?
  • Do your need local/regional approval?
  • Are you working with limited?
If any of these are true a centralized package authority may not meet your needs.

In this module, you will learn how we can use multiple package authorities (Habitat Builder/Depot) to provide both centralized and decentralized control over your environments.
