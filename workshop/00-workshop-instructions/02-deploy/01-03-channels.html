Builder Channels

Chef Habitat Builder's packages are exposed via Channels. A single package can be in multiple channels, when the Habitat Supervisor loads a package they indicate which package and channel to track.

Channels can be used to indicate the enviornment (dev, qa, staging, production) or they can be used to indicate a distribution channel (region1, region2, store1, store2). For a greater degree of flexibility multiple Builders, Origins and Channels can be used together to build custom distribution systems that match your business needs.
