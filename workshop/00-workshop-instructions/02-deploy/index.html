Promotion and Rollback

When managing deployments spread across multiple environments/locations controlling the delivery (promotion) of applications to those environments is important. However, equally as important is how to revoke (rollback) a delivered application to one or more environments.

In this module, you will learn how to use a centralized package authority (Habitat Builder/Depot) to control the entire SDLC lifecycle. Allowing your team to develop and deploy applications after and faster to all your environments.
