Workshop Design

The Habitat Edge Management workshop is designed to simulate multiple edge management scenarios. The deployment includes

  • 1x Developer Workstation
  • 2x Automate Servers w/
    • Chef Infra Server
    • Habitat Builder
  • 3x Virtual Stores
    • Store 1 w/
        Store 1 - Device 1
        Store 1 - Device 2
    • Store 2 w/
        Store 2 - Device 1
    • Store 3 w/
        Store 3 - Device 1
        Store 3 - Device 2
