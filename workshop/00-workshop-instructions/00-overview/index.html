Overview

In this Edge Managemen workshop, through a mix of feature/service explanation, videos and hands-on labs led by Progress Chef, you will learn about the foundational features/services in the Progress Chef Edge Management solution.

The hands-on labs are largely divided into basic and advanced modules.

  • In basic modules, you can learn various foundational features of each service.
  • In advanced modules, you can learn how to connect each these featuers and service to create architecture unique to your business requirements.
